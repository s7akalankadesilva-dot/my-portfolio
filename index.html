Bio

Suchintha Akalanka De Silva

IT Undergraduate · SLIIT, Sri Lanka

I'm an IT undergraduate at the Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT), passionate about the intersection of cutting-edge technology and creative digital strategy. My academic journey is driven by curiosity — from designing intelligent systems to crafting compelling digital narratives.

On the creative-tech side, I specialise in AI Prompt Engineering — designing precise prompts that unlock the full potential of generative AI tools — and AI Animation, bringing ideas to life through AI-assisted motion and visual storytelling. My eye for aesthetics is backed by hands-on experience in Graphic Editing, from brand visuals to polished social content.

My Digital Marketing and Content Strategy expertise means I understand how to make technology connect with real audiences — growing engagement, building communities, and translating complex ideas into content people actually care about.

I'm always building, always learning, and always looking for the next challenge that blends code with creativity.
